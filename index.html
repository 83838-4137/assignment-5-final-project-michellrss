SANTIAGO REYES

Santiago Reyes is a remarkable individual who embodies the American dream. Born in 1975 in the town of Guanajuato, Mexico, he immigrated to the United States at the age of 10, eventually making Houston, Texas, his hometown. A proud alumnus of the University of Texas, where he majored in business management, Santiago's entrepreneurial spirit and vision were truly ignited after a life-altering diagnosis of stage 3 lung cancer at the age of 38. During a two-year journey of recovery, Santiago found the inspiration to start his luxury watch company, Insight, which has since become a symbol of his tenacity and creativity.

TIMELESS INNOVATION

TIMELESS INNOVATION

Introducing the 1st collection by Insights called "Eteréo", which is a testament to the indomitable spirit of Santiago Reyes. Born from Santiago's profound journey through illness and spirituality, Eteréo encapsulates the timeless essence of celestial harmony and personal transformation. This remarkable collection consists of six exquisite timepieces, each a masterpiece in its own right. The signature feature of these watches is their celestial intricacy, showcasing both astrological signs and the mesmerizing dance of moon phases. Santiago's resilience and newfound wisdom inspire every detail of this collection, making Eteréo an embodiment of hope, elegance, and the enduring connection between the universe and the human spirit.
